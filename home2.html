The Process

From Wood
To Soul

Every character begins as a block of lime wood or a sketch on a napkin. We follow a rigorous process of anatomical study and mechanical engineering to ensure every joint moves with intent.

Our studio in London is a blend of a dusty carpenter's workshop and a high-tech electronics lab, where the old worlds and new technologies coexist.
