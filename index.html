The Artisan

Twenty Years of
Living Theater

I'm Marcus Vale, a master puppeteer who has dedicated his life to the ancient craft of bringing inanimate forms to life. From Javanese shadow puppets to complex foam-latex marionettes, every creation tells a story.

My work has graced stages across 34 countries, earned 12 international awards, and touched millions through television and film — yet every performance begins with the same quiet ritual: listening to what the puppet wants to say.

The Craft Behind
Every Character

01

Hand-Sculpted

Every puppet is individually sculpted, cast, and painted by hand using traditional and contemporary materials.

02

Narrative First

Characters are built from their stories outward — personality shapes physicality before a single tool is raised.

03

Technical Mastery

Cable mechanisms, animatronics, and precision rod systems give each puppet an authentic, fluid presence.

04

Cultural Heritage

Deep study of Wayang, Bunraku, Commedia, and shadow traditions informs every new creation's soul.

05

Collaborative

Working closely with directors, writers, and clients to realise their vision through the language of puppetry.

06

Global Reach

Performances and workshops delivered in 34 countries, with multilingual capability and cultural sensitivity.
